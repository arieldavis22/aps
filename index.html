Hi! I'm Amauriah, most people call me Mars. I’m a classically trained actress and singer with a passion for multicultural reconciliation and healing. I live for complex narratives and evocative roles. My promise is to share the truth in every performance—whether on stage or in front of the camera—creating memorable and moving human experiences.
